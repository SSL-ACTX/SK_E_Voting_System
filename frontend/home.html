SK e-Voting System

The SK e-Voting System is an online platform designed to facilitate electronic voting for the Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) elections. It allows registered voters to cast their votes securely and conveniently from any location with internet access. The system ensures transparency, accuracy, and integrity of the electoral process, promoting greater participation and engagement among youth voters.

Key features of the SK e-Voting System include:

  • Secure user authentication and authorization
  • Encrypted transmission of votes
  • Real-time monitoring and reporting
  • Accessibility features for voters with disabilities
